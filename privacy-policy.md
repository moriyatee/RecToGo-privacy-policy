# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** June 29, 2025

moriyatee (hereinafter referred to as "we" or "us") has established this Privacy Policy (hereinafter referred to as "this Policy") regarding the handling of users' personal information in the application "RecToGo" (hereinafter referred to as "this App") provided by us.

## Article 1 (Information Collected and Purpose of Use)

This App collects and uses the following information for the respective purposes:

### 1. Google User Information
- **Information Collected:** Email address and profile information of Google account
- **Collection Method:** Through Google's authentication screen when the user grants permission
- **Purpose of Use:** To identify users and authenticate access to Google Drive.

### 2. Google Drive Data
- **Information Collected:** Metadata about files and folders in the user's Google Drive (file names, folder names, etc.), file content (audio recording data)
- **Collection Method:** Through user operations using this App's functions
- **Purpose of Use:**
  - To upload audio recording data to Google Drive.
  - To display a list of folders on Google Drive so users can select a save destination.
  - To create new save destination folders on Google Drive.

This App does not use user data for advertising purposes or sell it to third parties.

## Article 2 (External Transmission of Information and Provision to Third Parties)

This App transmits user information to Google within the scope necessary to achieve the purposes specified in Article 1, as follows:

- **Recipient:** Google LLC
- **Information Transmitted:** Information specified in Article 1
- **Purpose of Transmission:** To upload files and perform folder operations using the Google Drive API.

For Google's Privacy Policy, please refer to the following link:  
[https://policies.google.com/privacy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

Except as described above and as required by law, we will not provide personal information to third parties without user consent.

## Article 3 (Security Measures)

We will take necessary and appropriate measures to prevent leakage, loss, or damage of personal information we handle, and for the security management of personal information. This App uses the API in accordance with Google's security standards and strives to handle user data safely.

## Article 4 (Access Permissions)

When using the Google Drive API, this App requests only the minimum necessary scope (`https://www.googleapis.com/auth/drive`) to implement its functions. This permission is used only for uploading recording files to folders specified by users and for selecting and creating save destination folders.

## Article 5 (Changes to Privacy Policy)

We may change this Policy as necessary. However, if changes to this Policy require user consent under applicable laws, the changed Policy will apply only to users who have consented to the changes in the manner prescribed by us. When changing this Policy, we will notify users of the effective date and content of the changed Policy by displaying it on our website or other appropriate means.

For opinions, questions, complaints, or other inquiries regarding the handling of user information, please contact us at the following:

End of Policy
