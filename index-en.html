About RecToGo

RecToGo is an iOS application that offers high-quality audio recording, multiple format options, 1-tap uploads to Google Drive or iCloud, and user-friendly playback controls.

Developed for personal use, RecToGo offers a simple, user-friendly interface with reliable cloud integration features.

Easy Cloud Upload

  • Simply tap the cloud icon on the left to upload your recordings to the cloud
  • With the Pro plan, enable auto-upload before recording to automatically upload after recording is complete
  • Compatible with both Google Drive and iCloud, allowing you to choose your preferred cloud service

Key Features

High-Quality Audio Recording

Record high-quality audio with a simple tap. Perfect for meetings, lectures, and quick notes.

Audio Format Selection

Choose from multiple audio formats (.m4a, .wav) to balance sound quality and file size.

1-Tap Cloud Upload

Seamlessly upload your recordings to Google Drive or iCloud with just one tap for easy sharing and backup.

Advanced Playback

Features playback controls including play, pause, fast-forward, and rewind to efficiently listen to your recordings.

Simple UI

Intuitive and easy-to-use interface. Record, play, and manage your audio files effortlessly.

Pricing Plans

Free Plan

Up to 5 cloud uploads per week. Access to all basic recording and storage features.

Pro Plan

Unlimited cloud uploads and automatic sync functionality. Enhanced recording experience.
