About RecToGo

RecToGo is an iOS application that provides functionality to record audio and save it to your cloud storage services. Designed with privacy as the top priority, the developer never accesses your recording content.

Developed for personal use, RecToGo offers a simple, user-friendly interface with reliable cloud integration features.
